# 15 — Member Platform

## Purpose

The member platform is the daily operating experience for fitness progress.

## Core Modules

- Onboarding
- Home Dashboard
- Today's Plan
- Workout Engine
- Nutrition
- Progress
- Recovery
- Coach Messaging
- Community
- Challenges
- Rewards
- Profile
- Settings
- Notifications
- Calendar

## Primary Experience

A member should open GymCord and immediately know what to do today. The app should guide action, reduce decision fatigue, and create momentum.

## Required Capabilities

- Personalized onboarding under five minutes
- Pre-populated dashboard after onboarding
- Workout preview, execution, logging, and summary
- Nutrition goals, meal logging, and plan guidance
- Progress photos and metric tracking
- AI and trainer messages
- Missions, streaks, achievements, and XP
- Push notifications that are useful rather than noisy

## Data Collected

Goal, experience level, schedule, equipment, nutrition preferences, limitations, measurements, workout logs, meals, photos, messages, missions, XP, achievements, and behavioral events.

## Success Metrics

Daily active users, onboarding completion, workout starts, workout completion rate, first-week retention, mission completion, AI engagement, coach message response, and 30-day retention.
