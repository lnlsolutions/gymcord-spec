# 17 — Gym Platform

## Purpose

The gym platform gives fitness businesses operational control over members, staff, schedules, billing, reporting, marketing, and retention.

## Core Modules

- Gym Dashboard
- Staff Management
- Memberships
- Scheduling
- Billing
- Reporting
- Marketing
- CRM
- Multi-location
- Equipment
- Point of Sale

## Primary Experience

A gym owner should see business health at a glance and act quickly on members, staff, payments, leads, and retention signals.

## Required Capabilities

- Member directory and status tracking
- Staff roles and permissions
- Membership plan management
- Class and appointment scheduling
- Billing visibility
- Operational reporting
- Campaign and announcement tools
- Lead CRM
- Multi-location support
- Equipment tracking
- POS-ready architecture

## Multi-Tenant Requirements

Every gym belongs to an organization. Data must remain scoped by organization and gym unless cross-location permissions are explicitly granted.

## Success Metrics

Weekly active gym admins, retained members, payment recovery, schedule utilization, lead conversion, staff engagement, announcement engagement, and churn-risk reduction.
