# 16 — Trainer Platform

## Purpose

The trainer platform helps coaches manage more clients with higher quality and less administrative work.

## Core Modules

- Trainer Dashboard
- Client Management
- Workout Builder
- Exercise Library
- Nutrition Builder
- Progress Reviews
- Messaging
- Calendar
- CRM
- Lead Management
- Business Analytics
- AI Assistant

## Primary Experience

A trainer should understand who needs attention, what clients are doing, what progress is happening, and what actions to take next.

## Required Capabilities

- Client roster and profile management
- Program creation and assignment
- Workout editing and progression rules
- Exercise library management
- Nutrition guidance and templates
- Progress review workflows
- Messaging and check-ins
- Calendar scheduling
- Lead tracking and client conversion
- Revenue and retention visibility
- Atlas assistant for programming, messaging, and analysis

## Permissions

Trainers can access clients assigned to them or authorized by the gym. Private member data must respect organization, gym, and consent settings.

## Success Metrics

Weekly active trainers, client response rate, program assignment rate, workout adherence, progress review completion, retained clients, lead conversion, and trainer revenue visibility.
